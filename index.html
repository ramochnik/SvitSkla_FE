    Framed Mirror Questions and Answers

    Can you make a mirror to match my custom measurements?

    Yes! Our framed mirrors are made to order and tailored to your measurements. After we receive your overall size measurements (from you or our install team), we will adjust the standard size to perfectly match your space.

    We will always make your mirror smaller, or up to 4” taller at no additional cost. Should you require a wider mirror than selected, we suggest choosing the next available size. If you purchase a size smaller than required, we will charge the difference between the original selection and the next size up.

    I have questions. Can I talk to someone about this?

    Absolutely! Call one of our friendly Hall of Frames associates at 602-357-2252.

    What if my measurements don’t fit a standard size?

    No problem! We will tailor your framed mirror to your measurements. Depending on the size adjustments needed, additional charges may apply.

    Can I talk to a designer before I buy?

    Of course! We want you to be 100% confident in your purchase. Give us a call at 602-357-2252, visit a store, or tell us about your dream mirror here!

    Can I provide my own mirror?

    For our online framed mirror products, no. If you would like to use your own mirror, please visit one of our 7 retail locations in the Phoenix Metro area.

    Can you remove my existing mirror glued to the wall?

    Unfortunately, we don’t offer that service. If your mirror is not actually glued to the wall, we usually dispose of it. For mirror removal, we recommend the following companies:

    Cohaco
    True Vue Glass

    Can you make a frame to go over my existing mirror?

    No, we do not offer that product at this time. We recommend you get your mirror removed and purchase a new framed mirror that perfectly fits your space.

    How does shipping work?

    We offer free mirror delivery within the Phoenix metro area. If you are located outside greater Phoenix, we can ship mirrors via crate shipping. Please contact us online or call us at 602-357-2252 for a crate shipping quote.

    Is delivery and installation included?

    Local Phoenix metro area delivery is included in your purchase; you may also pick your mirror up in store. Want us to hang it too? We are happy to install your framed mirror for an additional fee. Starting at $120, for up to 3 framed mirrors.

    Additional charges will apply for over 3 framed mirrors and zip codes out of our standard service area.

    Can I pick up my mirror at a Hall of Frames store?

    Absolutely! You can pick up your framed mirror at any Hall of Frames or Paradise Picture Frame location in Arizona at no additional cost.

    Will you measure my space for me?

    Yes, we offer free measurement appointments. Our expert installers will visit your home and gather accurate measurements to help you build the perfect framed mirror for your space. If you would like to schedule a measurement appt before you buy, please submit a form here.

    Do you have other frame colors or styles available?

    Yes, we have 1000+ frame options available in our stores. Please visit one of our 7 retail locations or tell us about your dream framed mirror to learn more!

    Can I order a frame sample?

    Yes, we offer free frame moulding samples for all 6 frames available online. Order today and pickup in-store or ship to your home for a $5 flat rate shipping fee. Shipping fee will be deducted from your final framed mirror purchase.

    Can I return my made to order framed mirror?

    All made to order framed mirrors are non-returnable, except in the case of a defective product. Please order a sample or visit a store before you order to ensure you’re 100% happy with your design.
